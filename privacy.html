More About Cookies

A cookie is a piece of information that is stored on your computer's hard drive by your web browser. This can allow you to experience a more personalised service when you revisit this website and allows us to find out about your last visit. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but usually you can alter the settings of your browser to prevent automatic acceptance if you prefer. If you choose not to receive our cookies, you may not be able to access or use all parts of this website.
